Egypt Visa Info



Notice: In case of Delay/Rejection Gulliver's Travel Services is not to be held responsible.

*  One Fully, Correctly & Clearly, Completed Visa Application.


*  Covering Letter a Request of Issuance of Visa.


*  Photocopy of National Id Card (Nic) A4 Size/Birth Certificate (If Children's Accompany).


*  A Passport Valid Until at Least Six Months (06)


*  A Confirmed Returned Air Ticket.


*  Polio Vaccination Certificate.


*  6 Months Latest Bank Statement with Account Maintaining Certificate.


*  Hotel reservation and Fax Intimation to The Embassy (Attention: Consulate Section Fax # 2279552) by The Hotel Concerned Confirming Your Stay in Egypt.



Attention Visa Applicants


*  Applications and Required Documents Should be Submitted at Least Three (03) Week Before the Intended Departure Date.


*  Applicants are Requested to Appear in Person to Submit the Applications.


*  Passports May be Collected By A Third Person.


*  The Embassy May Request Additional Information and/or Documents at Any Time.


*  All Fax Massages/Letters Pertaining to Visa Matters Should Be Addressed to : To Consular Section Fax #: 2279552.


*  Applicants Must Specify Immediate Contact Number or Mobile Number in Case an Interview is Required or an Additional Document is Needed.


*  The Consular Section Takes No Responsibility if A Passport/Documents is Not Collected Within Two (02) Weeks Time After the Issuance of Visa Receipt.


*  Supplying the Documents Does Not Automatically Guarantee That the Visa Will Be Granted.


*  Consular Section Workings Hours are from Monday to Friday 10:00 h to 12:00 h and Collection of Passports and Documents: Monday to Friday 02:30 h to 03:30 h Only.

Visa Fees & Processing Time

Visa fees:                  Rs. 15,500/-

Services charges:     Rs. 4000/-

Processing time:       30-60 Working Days minimum.

Submission Address:   Bahria Complex 4, 4th Floor Chaudhry Khaliq-uz-Zaman Road, Gizri.

Collection Day & Time :   Monday to Friday 9:00 am to 5:00 pm.

Ramadan Submission Day & Time :   Monday to Friday 9:00 am to 1:00 pm.
