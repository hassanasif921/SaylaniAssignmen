About Poznan


Alternative Title : Posen

Poznań, German Posen, city, capital of Wielkopolskie województwo (province), west-central Poland, located on the Warta River near its confluence with the Cybina.
Poznan is a good base for sightseeing in Greater Poland, and you will find many outstanding places to visit in nearby towns. Among these are Gniezno, the first capital of Poland, Kornik with its magnificent castle, Rogalin where the oldest oaks in Poland grow and Wolsztyn with its steam railway. Poznan gives you the opportunity relax in one of its numerous parks or near the artificial Malta Lake, a popular recreation area of Poznan inhabitants and tourists.
